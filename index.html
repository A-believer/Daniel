Principal Investigator: Tashiana Roberts-Jackson

University of Texas at Arlington College of Nursing

Contact Information: Tashiana.Roberts@mavs.uta.edu

The purpose of this research study is to examine the relationship

between structural empowerment and intent to stay among NPD

Practitioners amid the COVID-19 pandemic.

Are you a hospital-based nurse educator?

If so, we want to hear from you.

To participate in this research study, you must:

  • Currently work as an NPD practitioner or hospital-based nurse educator in a non-supervisory role in the acute care hospital setting
  • Have at least 3 months experience in your role
  • Work Full-time and work less than 50% of your time in direct patient care

Participation in this study involves:

  • Completion of a short online survey.
  • The survey will take less than 10 minutes to complete.
